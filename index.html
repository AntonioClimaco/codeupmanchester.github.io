CodeUp is about free adult coding tuition for everyone. We offer a friendly space where anyone, from complete beginners to accomplished software engineers, can get together, learn and share a passion for coding.
Our sessions take place on the second Monday of every month at Code Computerlove. Everybody is welcome, regardless of coding ability or preferred technologies. Head to the event page page to sign up now. It's completely free!
We're always looking to improve. If you have any feedback, good or bad, please use our feedback form.

CodeUp Manchester is sponsored by Northcoders, the North's original coding school. Nestled in the heart of Manchester, they combine highly personalised tuition and career support with that unique Northern community, spirit and energy.
